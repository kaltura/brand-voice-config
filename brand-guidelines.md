# Kaltura Brand Guidelines

## Identity

**Brand name:** Kaltura (always capitalize; never "kaltura" or "KALTURA")

**Mission:** Powering agentic, AI-driven digital experiences.

**Core values:** Open · Flexible · Collaborative.


## Tone & Voice

- Person: second
- Voice: active
- Sentences: max 25 words
- Contractions: yes
- Write with enthusiasm — confident, not breathless
- Show genuine empathy; avoid corporate distance
- No jargon, business-speak, or filler words


## Vocabulary

**Always use:** Kaltura, Content Hubs, LMS Extensions, Rich Media CMS, Kaltura Room, Agentic DX Platform, Agentic Avatars, Genies, Content Lab, Live Studio, Interactive Video Paths, Agentic Workflows, Conversational Agents, Kaltura Events & Webinars, Kaltura Player

**Avoid:** leverage, utilize, synergy, seamless, cutting-edge, robust, innovative, game-changing, simply, easy, users, KMS, KAF, KMC, KME, EP, Newrow, Mediaspace, chatbots

**Forbidden:** "AI Content Lab", "Video Portal", "Events Platform", "Management Console", "KAF", "KME", "Newrow", "NR2"


## Visual Identity

- Primary color: #282828
- Secondary color: #006efa
- Accent color: #ff9dff
- Background color: #f8f8f5
- Text color: #282828
- Heading font: Centra No.1
- Body font: Source Sans Pro


## On-Tone Examples

> Kaltura gives your team one place to create, manage, and share video — without the complexity.

> Your learners get a smarter experience. You get one less thing to manage.

> Rich media experiences that feel human, move fast, and scale without limits.

## Off-Tone Examples

> Leverage our robust, innovative platform to seamlessly optimize your video workflows.

> Users can utilize the system to achieve their goals.

> Our AI capabilities work to synergize your content strategy.


## Quick Reference

1. **Voice:** Energy + Clarity + Warmth — upbeat, direct, human
2. **Always say "you"** — never "users" or "customers" in running copy
3. **Active voice, short sentences** — no passive constructions, no filler
4. **Canonical names matter** — use Content Hubs not KMS; LMS Extensions not KAF; Rich Media CMS not KMC
5. **Ban list:** leverage, utilize, synergy, seamless, robust, innovative, cutting-edge, game-changing, simply, easy
