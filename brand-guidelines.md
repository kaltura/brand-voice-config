# Kaltura Brand Guidelines

## Identity

**Brand name:** Kaltura (always capitalize; never "kaltura" or "KALTURA")

**Mission:** Powering rich, agentic digital experiences.

**Core values:** Open · Flexible · Collaborative

**Brand attributes:** Energy · Clarity · Warmth


## Tone & Voice

- Person: second
- Voice: active
- Sentences: max 25 words
- Contractions: yes
- Write with enthusiasm — confident, not breathless
- Show genuine empathy; avoid corporate distance
- No jargon, business-speak, or filler words
- Speak directly to the reader ("you"), never "users" or "customers" in running copy


## Vocabulary

**Always use:** Kaltura, Agentic DX Platform, Content Hubs, Rich Media CMS, LMS Extensions, Kaltura Room, Agentic Avatars, Genies, Content Lab, Live Studio, Interactive Video Paths, Agentic Workflows, Conversational Agents, Kaltura Events & Webinars, Kaltura Player, Virtual Classroom, CMS Extensions, Avatar Videos, Avatar Video Studio, Video-to-Live Agents, Kaltura Video Editor, Express Capture, Personal Capture

## Terminology & Product Naming

Use these terms consistently in all internal and external communications. Legacy/incorrect terms are listed alongside the correct replacement.

### The Platform

| Term | Short Form | Definition |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Agentic Digital Experience Platform | Agentic DX Platform | Kaltura's overall platform. Cloud-based; enables organizations to create, manage, and deliver video and rich media experiences. Supports AI-assisted and agentic experiences on a modular, API-first architecture. |

Three layers:
1. **Rich Media Content Creation** (short: Content Creation) — Tools to create, generate, and produce video and rich media.
2. **Rich Media Content Management** (short: Content Management) — Securely manages live and on-demand video and rich media assets, with centralized control of content, users, permissions, metadata, workflows, and analytics.
3. **Rich Agentic Experiences** (short: Agentic Experiences) — Applications and agentic experiences end users interact with directly.

### Other Products & Features

- **DIY Webcast** — Self-produced live broadcast using one-way live streaming. Preset template capability within Kaltura Events.
- **Express Capture** — Software-based screen, webcam, and audio recording for content creation.
- **Hold Your Own Key (HYOK)** — Security capability where customers manage their own encryption keys.
- **KAF** — Kaltura Application Framework. LMS integration layer. Use the solution name (LMS Extensions) in customer-facing contexts.
- **KAI** — Kaltura AI (internal name). Centralizes most AI services. Use the product name in customer-facing contexts.
- **KES** — Kaltura Edge Server. Proxy cache for eCDN video delivery.
- **Kino** — Kaltura's internal Content Hub for Kalturians.
- **KMS** — Kaltura MediaSpace. The front-end application for Content Hubs, Events & Webinars, and LMS Extensions. Use the solution name in customer-facing contexts; KMS only in technical/architectural discussions.
- **Kaltura Player** — Enterprise-grade video and audio player. v2 is deprecated; customers migrate to v7.
- **Personal Capture** — Desktop tool for recording video and screen content.
- **REACH** — Kaltura's marketplace for add-on services (e.g., Verbit for captioning).
- **Theming** — Backend function in KMS Admin for selecting Content Hubs or legacy version.
- **VPaaS** — Video Platform as a Service. Kaltura's underlying video infrastructure and APIs.
